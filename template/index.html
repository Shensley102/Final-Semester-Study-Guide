How it works & hotkeys

  • Start: Pick a Module, choose a Length, then click Start Quiz.
  • Answering: Click an option or press a letter key A–Z to toggle the option.
  • De-select: For single-select questions, pressing the same letter again unselects it.
  • Submit / Next: Press Enter to submit. Then the green button turns blue and becomes Next.
  • After you submit: You’ll see Correct/Incorrect, the Correct Answer, and the Rationale.
  • Results: End of run shows a summary and a review list with answers and rationales.
  • Reset: A red Reset button shows during an active quiz. It clears progress.
