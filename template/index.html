How it works & hotkeys
  • Start: Pick a Module, choose a Length, then click Start Quiz.
  • Answering: Click an option or press a letter key A–Z to toggle the option with that label.
  • De-select: For single-select questions, pressing the same letter again unselects it.
  • Submit / Next: Press Enter to submit. After submitting, the green Submit button turns blue and becomes Next. Press Enter again to go next.
  • After you submit: You’ll see Correct/Incorrect, the Correct Answer, and a short Rationale. Options are locked until you go Next.
  • End of run: You’ll get a summary plus a review list with answers and rationales.
  • Reset: A red Reset button shows in the top-right only during an active quiz. It clears progress and returns you here.
